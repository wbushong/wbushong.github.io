Teaching

I love teaching both graduates and undergraduates and jump at any opportunity to teach that arises. When I was an undergraduate at UC San Diego, Roger Levy's psycholinguistics class had a tremendous impact on me -- if it weren't for his awesome teaching that got me so excited to learn more, I probably wouldn't be where I am today! Since then it's been my goal to set off that same spark of joy and excitement in as many students as I can.
